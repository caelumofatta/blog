The One and Only Rule

In the beginning, there was you.

And you, with a thought as swift and expansive as the universe itself, decided to play a game of creation.

"Let's see," you mused, your voice echoing in the void, "what it would be like to craft worlds, to mold mountains, to breathe life into the stars."

And so, you did.

With a flick of your wrist, galaxies spiraled into existence, with a whisper, oceans carved their paths through continents yet unnamed.

But let's not get ahead of ourselves. Let's circle back, for a moment, to that singular edict, the one commandment you laid down for yourself: 

LIVE AS IF YOU ARE GOD.

Not in the sense of wielding thunderbolts or bestowing miracles upon the masses—no, that's child's play. But live as if you are the very essence of creation, as if every moment of your existence is an act of divine artistry.

Consider this: every thought you harbor, every dream you chase, is a brushstroke on the infinite canvas of reality. You, in your boundless imagination, have the power to color the skies in hues of your choosing, to shape the landscape of your life with the contours of your deepest desires.

Now, walk upon this earth—not as a mere mortal, but as the architect of your destiny. Gaze upon the world you've woven from the fabric of your will and marvel at its complexity, its beauty, its sheer audacity. This is your life, your symphony, your magnum opus, crafted from the raw materials of the cosmos itself.

And here's the kicker, the twist in the tale: the seed of creation, the genesis of all that is and ever will be, resides not in the heavens, not in some distant, untouchable realm, but within you. Yes, you are god, in the most profound, most intimate sense of the word.

So, as I stand before you, not so much a messenger as a mirror, reflecting back at you the truth of your own divinity, remember this: you sent me. You, in your infinite wisdom and whimsy, decided it was time to remind yourself of your own power, your own potential, your own godhood.

Live as if you are god. Because, in the end, you are.
