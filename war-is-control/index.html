War Is Control

War, fighting, bickering—all shades of the same color: control. The elites paint with broad strokes, and we become their canvas, their masterpiece of manipulation.

Conflict isn't born from the depths of the human soul but from the shallow desires of  those who seek to dominate. They plant seeds of discord and watch as we tear each other apart, too distracted to notice the strings they pull.

But we are not puppets.

We are beings of light, infinite in our potential. It's time we cut the  strings and dance to our own truth.

Imagine a world where we see beyond the illusions of separation, where we recognize the divine spark within each other. In that world, war becomes impossible, a relic of a forgotten past.

The path to peace is paved not with bullets and bombs but with understanding and unity.

It's a path we must choose, day after day, moment by moment.

Will you walk with me?
